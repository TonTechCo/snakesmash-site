Snake Smash 3D

Support

Need help with Snake Smash 3D? We're happy to help.

Contact us

Email info@tontech.co and we'll get back to you. When reporting a problem, it helps to include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

Common topics

  • Purchases. Remove Ads, power packs, and snake unlocks are billed through your Apple ID. Manage purchases in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions (for any subscriptions) on your device, and restore non-consumable purchases from the in-app store screen.
  • Leaderboard & your data. Setting a nickname is optional; you can change or clear it anytime in Settings, or email us to remove your leaderboard entry. See our Privacy Policy.
  • Controls. Snake Smash supports tilt steering and an on-screen joystick. You can switch between them, and recalibrate tilt, in Settings.
  • Progress reset. Settings includes a "Reset to Level 1" option that restarts progression while keeping your coins, unlocked snakes, and purchases.

Response time

We aim to respond within a couple of business days.
