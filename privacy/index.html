Snake Smash 3D — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-14

This Privacy Policy explains what information Snake Smash 3D (the "app", operated by TonTech) collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Snake Smash 3D is a hyper-casual 3D snake arena game published by TonTech. For privacy questions, contact us at the address in §8.

2. Accounts and how you play

  • No account required. You can play without creating an account or providing your name, email, or phone number. Your progress is stored on your device.
  • Optional leaderboard nickname. If you choose to appear on the global leaderboard, you may set a display nickname. It is shown publicly next to your score. Please don't use personal information you don't want shown.

3. Information we collect

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS): collected only after you accept Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, and used by Google AdMob for ad personalization and measurement.
  • Leaderboard nickname & score: if you set a nickname and submit a score, we store that nickname together with your best length/score on our leaderboard server to display global rankings. No email, password, or account is involved.
  • Anonymous gameplay analytics: aggregate event counts (for example level start and level complete), used to measure overall feature adoption. No identifier ties these events to you personally.
  • Purchase state: which in-app items you own (such as Remove Ads), derived from Apple's receipt data. We never receive or store your payment details — Apple processes all payments.

We do not knowingly collect your real name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, microphone audio, or precise location.

4. How we use information

  • To run the game: track progress, rank the global leaderboard, and tune difficulty.
  • To show and measure advertising to players who have not purchased Remove Ads.
  • To validate in-app purchases.
  • To understand aggregate usage and improve the app.

5. Third-party services

We share the minimum data needed with service providers that process it on our behalf or under their own policies:

  • Google AdMob — advertising (banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads); may collect device identifiers and app-interaction data to select and measure ads. See Google's privacy policy and how Google uses information.
  • Apple — in-app purchases and App Store services. See Apple's privacy policy.
  • Our leaderboard server (operated by TonTech) — stores only the chosen nickname and score described in §3.

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Advertising and your choices

Players who have not purchased Remove Ads see ads served by Google AdMob, which may use a device identifier for ad targeting and measurement. You can limit ad tracking via your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and Apple Advertising). In the EU/EEA and UK, a consent form (Google's User Messaging Platform) lets you set your advertising preferences on first launch. Purchasing Remove Ads removes banners and interstitials; rewarded ads remain available on an opt-in basis only.

7. Data retention and your rights

  • Progress, coins, unlocked snakes, and settings are stored locally on your device; uninstalling the app removes them.
  • Leaderboard: your chosen nickname and best score are retained on our server until you change them in-app or ask us to delete them (§8).
  • Depending on where you live (e.g., EEA/UK under GDPR, California under CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact info@tontech.co.

9. Children

Snake Smash 3D is intended for a general audience, is rated 4+, and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The advertising integrated in the app is not designed for use by children under 13.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, noted in the app.
